About Me & Scholarly Interests

I am an Instructional Services Librarian at Pace University's Birnbaum Library, and recently completed an MA in Liberal Studies with a concentration in Digital Humanities at the CUNY Graduate Center.

My scholarly interests include exploring the intersection of information literacy instruction and critical pedagogy, and how that affects in-person and online info lit instruction. Before I went to library school I was a pastry chef. I am a quilter, a triathlete, and a cat owner.
